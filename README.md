# Phnom Penh Street Art: A Digital Archive

This project was developed in January 2026 as part of the **"Arts for Transformation"** J-term course at **New York University Abu Dhabi**. 

During a two-week immersive journey through Cambodia, our group of eleven students explored the intersection of local culture, history, and creative expression. Fascinated by the evolving urban landscape of Phnom Penh, three of us set out to document the city‚Äôs vibrant street art scene before these ephemeral works are lost to time or urban development.
